About

Diary of Jonathan Brickwell is a First Person Shooter game that tells a story of an explorer stranded on an island.

He boarded a ship with 24 of his colleagues on a mission financed by an unknown source to map, escavate and explore the newly discovered islands in the Pacific ocean.
